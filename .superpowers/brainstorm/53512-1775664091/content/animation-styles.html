Lyrics Animation Style on Visualizer

How should synced lyrics animate into view on top of the visualizer canvas?

A. Gravity Drop

Lines fall from the top with gravity easing — they decelerate as they reach the center, then gently fade out downward when the next line arrives. Like snowflakes or falling leaves.

B. Curved Fly-In

Lines fly in from alternating left/right sides along a Bézier curve path, settling at the center. Each new line pushes the previous one out with a mirrored curve. Feels dynamic and musical.

C. Karaoke Wipe

Text appears statically centered but a highlight "wipe" sweeps across each word in sync with the beat. Current word glows bright, past words dim, upcoming words are ghosted. Classic karaoke feel.

D. Hybrid: Curved Entry + Karaoke Wipe

Lines fly in with a curve (like B), but once settled, words highlight progressively (like C). Best of both worlds — dynamic entry with precise karaoke sync.
