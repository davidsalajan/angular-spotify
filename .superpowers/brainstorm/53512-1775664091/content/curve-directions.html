Curve Entry Direction Pattern

How should successive lyric lines enter the visualizer?

A. Alternating Left / Right

Predictable ping-pong rhythm. Line 1 from left, line 2 from right, repeat. Feels balanced and musical — like a visual metronome.

B. Semi-Random Directions

Lines enter from varied angles — top-left, bottom-right, upper-right, etc. Each curve is unique. Feels organic and surprising, like the music is painting the words.

C. Energy-Driven

Calm sections → gentle curves from sides. High energy → dramatic sweeps from corners with longer arc paths. The animation intensity matches the music's energy level.
